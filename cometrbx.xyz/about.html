About Us

How Comet Came to be.

We level the playing field for thousands of Gamers everyday.

Since 2020, We've made products that level the playing field for Thousands of Gamers everyday. Our Team has made waves over the industry through innovative tactics. Through an emphasis on beautiful Design and amazing Power. Our impact over the industry could not be understated.

Comet started as a team-up effort between a Designer and a Developer from 2 competing products. Combining the expertise of unique, passionately-built design, and power that you would never get from anything else. Our Team built Comet to make a product that was amazing in every way possible.

Comet is now one of the fastest growing products in our Market Segment. Thousands of Gamers use our products everyday to improve their Gaming Experience, to help them achieve their Goals. Through Comet, we've shared our passion over Design and User Experience, and through this Passion. We have influenced many people around the world.

We hope you enjoy Comet, and enjoy what's more to come.
